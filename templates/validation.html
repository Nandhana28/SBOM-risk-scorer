The honest trade-off. Two of the challenge's own success criteria — false-positive rate < 20% and vulnerability-detection recall > 85% — cannot both hold on this dataset, because its labels flag many low-severity and already-patched CVEs as risky. Rather than quoting one flattering operating point, the tool exposes three risk-appetite policies and reports every criterion for each. Choose the policy that matches your organisation's tolerance; the reasoning stays identical and fully auditable.
